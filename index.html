frequently asked questions

What support does StarCare provide to PRESCHOOL owners?

StarCare offers comprehensive support, including curriculum guidance, training, marketing assistance, parent engagement tools, and ongoing operational support to help franchise owners successfully manage their preschools.

What is the initial investment required to open a StarCare Preschool?

Starting a StarCare Preschool requires Zero investment , covering infrastructure, tech, branding, and training setup based on location and facility scale.

Do I need prior experience in education to open a StarCare Preschool?

No prior education experience needed; StarCare provides comprehensive training, equipping franchisees with essential knowledge for managing a preschool effectively.

What is the process for becoming a StarCare PRESCHOOL?

Submit an application, attend an evaluation, select an approved site, sign the agreement, and prepare for setup, training, and launch with StarCare’s guidance.

How long does it take to set up and launch a StarCare Preschool?

Setting up takes 2–3 months from agreement to launch, including site preparation, staff training, system integration, and promotional activities.

What ongoing assistance is provided after the preschool is launched?

StarCare provides continuous training, curriculum updates, operational support, quality checks, marketing assistance, and dedicated customer support after preschool launch.

Why Settle for a Franchise? – Launch Your Own Independent Preschool and Brand Your Way with StarCare Systems

With StarCare Systems, establish your preschool under your own brand and expand by offering franchises. StarCare provides everything you need for growth: zero initial investment, marketing support, legal guidance, admissions assistance, and an efficient management platform. Featuring the Discover Curriculum, your preschool can deliver high-quality education, create a unique identity, and scale confidently by sharing your success model with future franchisees.
